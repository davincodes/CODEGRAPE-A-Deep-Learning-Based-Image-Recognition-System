CODE GRAPE:A Deep Learning-Based Image Recognition System for Automated Detection of Black Measles Disease in Grape leafs

Black Measles

→

One of the common fungal diseases is Black Measles which is found in the Grape Plants and can be identified as brown streaking lesions on any part of the leaf. Symptoms first become apparent in vineyards 5 to 7 or more years old, but the infections actually occur in younger vines.

Black Rot

→

Black rot is one of the most damaging diseases of grapes, The disease is caused by the fungus Guignardia bidwellii. The fungus can infect the leaves, shoots, berries, tendrils, rachises and cluster stems (peduncles) of grapes.

Leaf Blight

→

Leaf Blight/Bacterial blight can affect the leaf, petiole, stem, root, shoot or flowers. Can be identified as yellow-green disease spots gradually appear on the fronts of the grape leaves with downy mildew, and white frosty mildew appears on the backs of the leaves.

Healthy leaf

→

A healthy leaf on grapevines typically refers to a leaf that exhibits normal characteristics and is free from disease, pests, or physiological disorders.

Service

Our image recognition system powered by Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) is implemented on a user-friendly web page, accessible to grape farmers and viticulturists. The web page allows users to upload images of their grape leafs and obtain an accurate diagnosis of any diseases present. The system's intuitive interface simplifies the image recognition process, enabling users to easily upload, classify, and obtain information on the diseases affecting their crops. Choose a photo that you want the system to recognize:


After choosing a photo our advanced image recognition system powered by Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) will promptly identify the specific disease present in the image of your grape crop. The system will then provide a detailed description of the identified disease to facilitate a better understanding of its characteristics and potential impact on crop yield and quality.In addition to the disease description, the system will offer a range of possible treatments for the identified disease based on current research and expert recommendations.

About the Researchers

We are students of the University of the Cordilleras trying to use both agricultural advancement technology and augmentation approaches to develop a dependable answer to the agricultural problems facing by grape farmers. Imagine a world where farmers and viticulturists no longer have to manually inspect their grape crops for signs of disease. Instead, they can simply snap a photo with their smartphone and use image recognition technology powered by Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to identify the disease present in the image.
